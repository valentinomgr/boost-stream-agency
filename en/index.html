TK Laser Meta Ads

Laser Epilation and Cosmetology Salon Network

Comprehensive advertising campaign setup for a network of 3 salons in Kyiv. Working with various procedures and price segments.

150-300 leads/month
$3-5 CPL
35% conversion to booking
2+ years collaboration

Challenge

  • Scale lead flow from Meta Ads while keeping CPL under $5
  • Split advertising campaigns by procedure types for more precise targeting
  • Reduce the percentage of no-answers and low-quality leads

What we did

  • Split campaigns by procedures — laser separately, facials separately, injections separately. Much easier to control CPL for each direction
  • Tested many creatives: before/after, procedure process, client reviews. Reviews worked best — CTR increased by 40%
  • Drove traffic to quiz landing page instead of regular form. People take the test, get results and leave contacts — conversion to booking much higher
  • Used Advantage+ Audience seeded with existing client base — algorithm quickly found similar audiences
  • Set up rules: if CPL rises above $7 — pause ad set, if below $4 — increase budget
  • Split retargeting into 7, 14 and 30 days with different offers and messages
  • Immediately disabled Audience Network — leads from there were just junk

Results

  • CPL dropped from $8-10 to $3-5 depending on procedure
  • Stable flow of 150-300 leads monthly with $1,500-2,000 budget
  • Lead Form → booking conversion increased from 22% to 35%
  • Share of low-quality leads decreased from 30% to 12%
Discountone Meta Ads + Google Ads

Multi-category Marketplace for Indian Market

Comprehensive performance advertising launch for a multi-category marketplace in one of the largest markets in the world — India. Over 1.4 billion potential customers.

5.8x ROAS
$0.8 CPC
$50K+ budget/month
12K+ orders/month

Challenge

  • Launch advertising campaigns in the Indian market from scratch and achieve ROAS 4x+
  • Build scalable account structure for 50K+ SKUs across different categories
  • Optimize for market specifics: cash on delivery, mobile traffic, low average order value

What we did

  • Built classic structure: prospecting to cold audiences, retargeting those who interacted, and DPA for those who viewed products
  • Launched Performance Max in Google separately for electronics, clothing and home goods — easier to optimize for different margins
  • Tested formats: carousel, static, video. Video won by a large margin — CTR +65%, CPA -20%
  • Tried different bidding strategies. Cost Cap gave the best control over conversion cost
  • Connected dynamic retargeting with a feed of 50K products. Split by price segments — ran cheaper products more aggressively
  • Set up staged retargeting: viewed product — one message, added to cart — another, didn't complete purchase — push with discount
  • Lookalike 1-3% based on buyers works better than interest targeting. Broad didn't work at all
  • Split campaigns into prepayment and cash on delivery — in India 70% pay upon receipt, different unit economics
  • Automated via rules: ROAS above 5x — scale, below 3x for three days in a row — pause

Results

  • ROAS grew from 2.1x to 5.8x over 4 months of optimization
  • CPC dropped from $1.4 to $0.8 through creative and audience optimization
  • Scaled budget from $10K to $50K/month while maintaining ROAS at 5.5x+
  • 12,000+ orders/month, 40% of which are repeat buyers through retargeting
  • Site conversion increased from 1.2% to 2.8% after A/B landing page tests
NordiHouse Meta Ads + Google Ads

Turnkey Modular Houses

Lead generation for expensive product worth $20-50K. Working with cold audiences, warming up through content and closing on consultations.

$100-150 CPL
12x ROAS
25-30 leads/month
8% conversion to sale

Challenge

  • Build lead generation funnel for expensive product with 2-4 week sales cycle
  • Find optimal balance between lead quality and CPL (target — CPL under $150 with 5%+ conversion to sale)
  • Launch advertising on Meta and Google with focus on search intent

What we did

  • In Google collected semantics for high-intent queries: "modular house price", "buy turnkey modular house" — people there are ready to buy
  • In Meta built three-stage funnel: first video for warming up, then lead gen for those who watched, and retargeting for those who didn't leave a request
  • Tested creatives: 3D renders, real photos and video tours of finished houses. Video tours gave 35% lower CPL
  • Tried different funnels: just landing page, Lead Form, WhatsApp. Landing page with quiz won — people answer questions and leave more thoughtful requests
  • Made retargeting sequential: watched 50% video — show one message, visited site — another, started filling form and dropped — push to complete
  • Launched Google Display remarketing with custom audiences — people who searched for land plots, interested in construction
  • Implemented lead scoring with client: evaluate budget, urgency, land availability. This way managers don't waste time on cold leads
  • Tested long landing with all projects vs short with top-3. Short one gave 25% higher conversion — people don't want to scroll

Results

  • CPL stabilized at $100-150 (Google: $120, Meta: $95)
  • Lead → sale conversion increased from 4% to 8% thanks to scoring and qualification
  • ROAS 12x — with $3K/month spend we get 2-3 sales at $35-40K each
  • Average deal cycle shortened from 4 to 2.5 weeks thanks to content warming
  • Google Search brings 40% of leads with highest conversion to sale (12%)
FurnitHome Meta Ads + Google Ads

Furniture Sales in European Markets

Comprehensive entry of Ukrainian furniture manufacturer into Polish and German markets. Localization, offer adaptation and building lead generation system from scratch.

€15-18 CPL
18% conversion to sale
80+ leads/month
€2.8K avg. order

Challenge

  • Launch advertising campaigns in Polish and German markets from scratch
  • Test different advertising channels and find optimal mix for furniture niche
  • Achieve stable lead flow with CPL under €20 and 10%+ conversion to sale

What we did

  • Launched separate campaigns for Poland and Germany — different languages, different creatives, different market specifics
  • In Google collected semantics in Polish and German: "meble na wymiar", "Möbel nach Maß", "szafy przesuwne". Conversion from search is highest
  • Tested creatives: beautiful interior photos, production photos, video reviews. Interior photos won — CPL 30% lower
  • Tested audiences: homeowners, recently moved, interest in interior design. Homeowners 35-55 worked best
  • Tested funnels: Lead Form, landing page, Messenger. Landing page with price calculator won — people calculate cost and leave request immediately
  • Set up retargeting for 30-day visitors, those who interacted with posts and watched 75% of video
  • Launched dynamic banners in Google Display — showing exactly the furniture categories the person viewed on site
  • Tagged everything with UTMs and integrated into CRM — we see which channel brings how many sales, not just leads
  • Tested different positioning: "from €1500", "Premium quality", "Free design project". The last option gave 45% higher CTR

Results

  • CPL dropped from €30 to €15-18 over 3 months of optimization (Meta: €14, Google: €22)
  • Lead → sale conversion increased from 10% to 18% thanks to qualification and follow-up chains
  • 80+ leads/month from both markets with €1,500/month budget
  • 10-12 closed deals/month with average order €2,800 = €28-33K revenue
  • Distribution: Poland 65% leads (lower CPL), Germany 35% (higher order value)
